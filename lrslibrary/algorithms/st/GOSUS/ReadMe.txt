This is a preliminary version of the implementation of

Jia Xu, Vamsi K. Ithapu, Lopamudra Mukherjee, James M. Rehg, Vikas Singh. 
GOSUS: Grassmannian Online Subspace Updates with Structured-sparsity. 
In Proceedings of International Conference on Computer Vision (ICCV) , December 2013.


To run this code, you will need VLFEAT installed.

Please download the data from and change the data path accordingly. demo1.m and demo2.m shows two settings.

 [1] L. Li, W. Huang, I. Y. H. Gu, and Q. Tian. Statistical modeling of complex backgrounds for foreground object detection. TIP, 13(11):1459?1472, 2004.
 [2] K. Toyama, J. Krumm, B. Brumitt, and B. Meyers. Wallflower: Principles and practice of background maintenance. In ICCV, 1999.

For questions and bug report, please contact jiaxu@cs.wisc.edu.
